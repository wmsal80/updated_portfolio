A Little About Me

Welcome to my portfolio page. This is my first attempt at a formal html document to showcase my skill set so please be patient. Over time it will reflect how much I am learning.

I started off in the nuclear navy, then it was time to join the civilian world. I was a maintenance supervisor for almost 10 years in a couple of industries. I worked in a coal plant, a nuclear plant, a steel mill, a steel forge, a stamping plant, and lastly, repairing durable medical equipment.

I decided to try my hand at web development because I had programmed in high school and college and really enjoyed it. Little did I know how much different this would be. It was difficult, but then anything worth having requires effort. I hope you'll have a look around and see how I'm progressing.
